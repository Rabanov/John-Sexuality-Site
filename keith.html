About Keith

Dr. E. Keith Bowers is an evolutionary ecologist and assistant professor at the University of Memphis. Amongst other topics, Keith is an expert in the area of bioacoustics, as he possesses considerable expertise in the analysis of audio from the “begging” signals of birds. Keith with help guide our team throughout the extraction and analysis of audio data.
