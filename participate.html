Interested in Participating?


We are looking for heterosexual romantic couples in the Greater Toronto Area who meet the following criteria:

  • Both you AND your partner are interested in participating
  • You and your partner live with one another, without any children or roommates
  • You and your partner consider your sex-life to be “regular”


If that sounds like you and your partner, email us at Sounds.Of.Satisfaction.Study@gmail.com, and our research team will coordinate to enroll you and your partner in the study.


From there, a snapshot of the process of participation looks like this:

  • We will contact you to schedule an in-person or skype-based “visitation”, so that we can confirm you meet our study’s inclusion criteria, and we can collect some basic information from you—we can’t just give out iPods without knowing who we are lending them to, or where to retrieve them from/drop off payment for participation
  • We will drop off an iPod and charging kit, with instructions of where to place it. At this time, we will also collect you and your partner’s consent to participate in our study; throughout this process we’ll answer any questions you have about the study.
  • We will then send you and your partner separate links to complete our background survey.
  • Each day, at the same approximate time, we will send you and your partner separate links to complete the brief daily survey.
  • Near the end of your participation, we will contact you to schedule pick-up of the iPod, debrief you, answer any remaining questions that you may have about how we will be using your data, and pay you for your participation


If you have questions about other aspects of participation, check out our FAQ.
